==================================
DELIVERY & PAYMENT BUSINESS RULES
==================================

1. ADVANCE PAYMENT (VERSEMENT)

When an Admin creates or edits a laptop product, an additional field must exist:

- Selling Price (Public Price)
- Advance Payment Amount (Versement)

Example:

Selling Price = 98,500 DA
Advance Payment = 20,000 DA

The customer only sees:
- Product Price: 98,500 DA
- Advance Payment Required: 20,000 DA

The remaining amount is paid upon delivery.

----------------------------------

2. ORDER STATUS: ADVANCE PAID

A new order status must exist:

- Reserved
- Confirmed
- Advance Paid
- Prepared
- Shipped
- Delivered
- Cancelled

When Admin changes status to "Advance Paid":

Remaining Amount =
Product Price - Advance Payment

Example:

Laptop Price = 98,500 DA
Advance Payment = 20,000 DA

Remaining Amount =
78,500 DA

The system automatically updates the amount due.

----------------------------------

3. DELIVERY RESTRICTION

For every laptop:

Hidden Calculation:

Net Laptop Price =
Laptop Price - Advance Payment

This value is NOT visible to customer.

Rule:

IF Net Laptop Price > 85,000 DA

THEN:

❌ Home Delivery Disabled

Only:

✅ Office Pickup (Bureau)

must be available.

Example:

Laptop Price = 110,000 DA
Advance = 20,000 DA

Net Price = 90,000 DA

Result:

Only Office Delivery allowed.

----------------------------------

4. LAPTOP DELIVERY FEES

ALGIERS (ALGER)

Office Delivery:
500 DA

Home Delivery:
800 DA

ALL OTHER WILAYAS

Office Delivery:
1000 DA

Home Delivery:
1500 DA

----------------------------------

5. MULTIPLE LAPTOPS

Delivery fee is calculated PER LAPTOP.

Example:

2 laptops
Alger Office

500 x 2

Total Delivery = 1000 DA

Example:

3 laptops
Oran Home

1500 x 3

Total Delivery = 4500 DA

----------------------------------

6. LAPTOP + ACCESSORIES

If cart contains:

- Laptop
- Accessories

System uses ONLY Laptop Delivery Rules.

Accessories do not generate extra delivery fees.

Example:

1 Laptop
3 Accessories

Delivery fee =
Laptop delivery fee only.

----------------------------------

7. ACCESSORIES ONLY

If cart contains accessories only:

Use dedicated accessory delivery table.

Accessory delivery fee depends on Wilaya.

Create table:

ACCESSORY_DELIVERY_FEES

Fields:

- Wilaya
- Office Fee
- Home Fee

Admin can modify prices.

System automatically uses this table.

----------------------------------

8. DELIVERY CALCULATION ENGINE

Checkout automatically calculates:

- Products Total
- Delivery Fees
- Discount
- Promo Code Reduction
- Advance Payment
- Remaining Amount

Formula:

Grand Total =
Products Total +
Delivery Fees -
Discounts

----------------------------------

9. CUSTOMER DISPLAY

Customer must see:

✔ Product Price
✔ Delivery Fee
✔ Advance Payment
✔ Grand Total
✔ Remaining Amount

Customer must NOT see:

❌ Net Laptop Price
❌ Internal calculations
❌ Admin costs

----------------------------------

10. ADMIN DASHBOARD

Admin can:

- Define Advance Payment
- Modify Advance Payment
- Force Delivery Type
- Override Delivery Restriction
  (SUPER ADMIN ONLY)

Admin Order Page must show:

- Product Price
- Advance Payment
- Remaining Amount
- Delivery Fee
- Total Order Value

----------------------------------

11. SUPER ADMIN PERMISSIONS

Only SUPER ADMIN can:

- Change delivery prices
- Change 85,000 DA threshold
- Edit wilaya delivery fees
- Override delivery restrictions
- View hidden net price calculations
